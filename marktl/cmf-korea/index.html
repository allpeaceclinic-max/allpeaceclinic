Who We Are

OUR IDENTITY

A Christian Community Growing into a Kingdom Movement

Christian Medical Fellowship Korea is a community of Christian healthcare professionals and future healthcare professionals who acknowledge the Lordship of Jesus Christ in the medical community.


We are an evangelical, interdenominational Christian movement committed to advancing the Kingdom of God through healthcare.
