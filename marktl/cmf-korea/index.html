DAEGYEONG CMF · CAMBODIA

"Friendship is a fundamental practice of Christian mission."

— Dana Robert, Missiologist

In 2014, Dr. Sanghyeob Lee unexpectedly met Dr. Kang Jae-myung — a medical missionary with Interserve serving at Hebron Hospital in Cambodia — at a mission gathering. Dr. Kang had a simple question: "Could you come alongside Cambodian medical students in their faith?"

In January 2015, the first Cam-Kor Medical Student Retreat was held. The Cambodian students — medical, nursing, and pharmacy students — were holding their faith in an environment full of real challenges. We had come to give. But we were the ones who received.

2015년 첫 캄한수련회 단체사진 · First Cam-Kor Retreat, Jan 2015

The retreats continued year after year. In 2016, the second retreat — "Running Nomad" — brought the two communities even closer together.

2016년 캄한수련회 "Running Nomad" · Second Cam-Kor Retreat, Jan 2016

The friendship deepened into long-term commitment. Dr. Lee Jin-hyung served as a short-term missionary at Hebron Hospital (GMP). That seed took root: today Dr. Kim Gyeong-jung serves as a long-term internist with MMC through OMF, walking alongside the Cambodian medical campus community. The students who worshipped beside us graduated — and from among them, a Cambodian CMF was born.

2020년 캄한수련회 — 강재명 의료선교사와 함께 · 2020 Cam-Kor Retreat with Rev. Kang Jae-myung

This year, we are grateful to God that this journey of friendship has led us all the way here — to ICMDA 2026, Jeju — together.

"우정은 기독교 선교의 기초적인 실천이다."

— Dana Robert, 선교학자

2014년, 이상협 의사는 선교현장에서 강재명 선교사를 우연히 만났습니다. 강재명 선교사는 인터서브(Interserve) 소속으로 캄보디아 헤브론병원에서 사역 중이었고, 조심스럽게 물었습니다: "캄보디아 의대생들의 신앙을 함께 돌봐주실 수 있을까요?"

2015년 1월, 첫 캄한수련회가 열렸습니다. 의대, 간호대, 약대 학생들이 함께 모였습니다. 신앙을 지키기 어려운 도전들이 많은 현장에서도 믿음을 붙잡고 있는 캄보디아 학생들의 모습은, 오히려 우리를 도전했습니다. 주러 갔다가, 받고 왔습니다.

수련회는 해마다 이어졌습니다. 2016년에는 두 번째 수련회 "Running Nomad"가 열렸고, 두 공동체는 더욱 가까워졌습니다. 이진형 선생은 헤브론병원(GMP)에서 단기선교사로 섬겼고, 그 씨앗이 자라 김경중 선생이 현재 MMC(OMF) 소속 내과 전문의 장기선교사로 캄보디아 의대 캠퍼스 사역을 함께 감당하고 있습니다.

우리와 함께 예배하던 학생들이 졸업했고, 그 중에서 캄보디아 CMF가 탄생했습니다. 이 우정의 여정이 이제 ICMDA 2026 제주까지 함께 이어지게 된 것을 하나님께 감사드립니다.

우리는 그저 친구가 되러 갔습니다.
그 우정 안에서 하나님이 일하셨습니다.

CMF KOREA · MONGOLIA

CMF Korea's Mongolia mission began in 1993 — physicians serving alongside Mongolian doctors in clinical settings, a quiet partnership built on shared work and shared trust. That first chapter lasted until 1997.

Then in 2011, a new season began. A team of 30 to 40 members started making the journey to Mongolia every summer — a structured, sustained commitment that has continued year after year. Medical service remained at the heart of the work, but a new vision was quietly taking shape.

In 2018, the team began investing specifically in Mongolian medical and dental students — gathering them for 2 to 3-day retreats each summer, encouraging them to form a faith community that could sustain itself beyond the team's annual visits.

It was not easy. Between retreats, there was no one who could lead Bible study in Mongolian. Financial pressures made it hard for students to keep meeting. The community was fragile.

But the team kept praying — and kept returning. They named their core vision clearly: to plant a Mongolian CMF. Everything was oriented around that goal.

In 2025, something shifted. Missionaries who could lead Bible study in Mongolian joined the work. Regular gatherings — Bible study, sharing, medical lectures — became possible for the first time. Between visits, the team stays connected through social media, sharing prayer requests monthly, reminding the students again and again: we are with you.

Thirty years of going.
A community is taking root.
